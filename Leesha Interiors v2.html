About LEESHA

Where Vision Meets Craftsmanship

Founded in 2023, LEESHA INTERIORS has grown into one of India's most trusted interior design firms. We believe that every home should tell a story — and we're here to make that story extraordinary with our signature phrase: Your Dream Home, the wonder of our design.

From sprawling villas to cozy apartments, our work blends functionality with aesthetic brilliance. We don't just design interiors; we curate lifestyles with precision and passion.
